Historical gameplay option

Some scenarios have a "historical gameplay option". If you click the button, the setup changes for that options will take effect. More details on how this affect play are presented under each scenario.

Summer 1941: The Barbarossa Campaign

Historical gameplay option: none
The full Eastern Front from beginning to bitter end.

Summer 1942: Fall Blau

Historical gameplay option: Drive for Oil is the first event in this scenario.
"Case Blue" was the German codename for its 1942 summer offensive.

Winter 1942: Operation Saturn

Historical gameplay option: none
The Soviets planned a winter campaign of continuous and highly ambitious offensive operations.

Summer 1943: Turning Point: Kursk

Historical gameplay option: The Soviet Initiative Combat on the first turn is in the South.
The Germans hoped to shorten their lines with pincers breaking through the Kursk salient.

Spring 1944: Destruction of Army Group Center

Historical gameplay option: The Soviet Initiative Combat on the first two turns (Spring and Summer) are in the North.
Operation Bagration was Germany's "most calamitous defeat".

Winter 1944: End of the Iron Dream

Historical gameplay option: The Soviet Initiative Combat on the first two turns (Winter and Spring) are in the North.
The last year of war in Europe.

No matter what scenario you pick you can choose to play to the end of the war or to the end-point of any scenario.

For example:

  • Summer 1941 to the end of the war
  • Spring 1944 to the end of the war
  • Summer 1941 to Spring 1944
  • Summer 1942 to Winter 1942
  • and so on...
